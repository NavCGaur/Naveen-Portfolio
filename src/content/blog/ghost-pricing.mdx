## Ghost CMS Development Services

I offer comprehensive development services across the entire Ghost CMS ecosystem, supporting both self-hosted setups (DigitalOcean, Hetzner, AWS) and Ghost Pro.

### 1. Ghost Website Setup & Deployment

A properly configured server helps improve site reliability, security, and long-term maintainability. I configure robust setups that run smoothly under load:

*   **Custom Server Installation:** Setting up Ghost CLI on Ubuntu VPS (Hetzner, DigitalOcean, Linode).
*   **Security & SSL Configuration:** Free Let's Encrypt SSL, Nginx reverse proxy configuration, and UFW firewall hardening.
*   **Newsletter & Transactional Email:** Resolving delivery issues by setting up Mailgun, Brevo, or other secure SMTP configurations.
*   **Performance Infrastructure:** Configuring Cloudflare, CDN delivery, and server-side caching.
*   **Backup Solutions:** Automating regular file and database backups to secure offsite storage.

---

### 2. Ghost Theme Customization

Make your site match your brand exactly. I specialize in modifying and customizing Ghost themes without causing future update headaches:

*   **Visual Enhancements:** Restructuring homepages, headers, and footer layouts.
*   **Custom Landing Pages:** Building standalone landing pages within your Ghost site for high-converting marketing campaigns.
*   **Advanced Layouts:** Injecting responsive sidebars, custom author boxes, sticky navigation, and styled sign-up forms.
*   **Membership & Newsletter Flows:** Optimizing signup overlays, membership tiers, and subscriber portals.
*   **Code Injection Tuning:** Safely applying custom styles and scripts using Code Injection to avoid breaking theme updates.

---

### 3. WordPress to Ghost Migration

Migrate your publication away from slow database queries and heavy plugin ecosystems without losing your search engine rankings:

*   **SEO Preservation:** Mapping high-value URLs to prevent 404 errors, maintaining clean redirect rules, and matching existing metadata.
*   **Content & Media Integrity:** Migrating all articles, authors, categories (mapped to tags), pages, images, and embedded media assets cleanly.
*   **Database Cleanup:** Stripping old WordPress shortcodes, broken HTML, and junk database values from your imported content.
*   **Subscriber Imports:** Transferring your existing email subscriber list into Ghost's native membership system seamlessly.

---

### 4. Performance & Technical SEO Optimization

Slow pages lose readers and rank poorly on Google. I audit and optimize your Ghost frontend to achieve peak scores:

*   **Core Web Vitals:** Optimizing Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), Interaction to Next Paint (INP), and Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS).
*   **Image Delivery:** Implementing modern formats (WebP/AVIF), responsive image source sets, and lazy loading.
*   **Asset Compression:** Minifying and consolidating CSS/JS payloads and optimizing web font loading behaviors.
*   **Technical SEO Hardening:** Ensuring semantic HTML5 structure, structured schema JSON-LD, clean sitemaps, and optimized search console tracking.

---

### 5. Integrations & Automations

Connect your Ghost publication to the rest of your marketing and sales stack:

*   **Analytics Platforms:** Implementing Google Analytics (GA4), Search Console, and privacy-first tracking utilities.
*   **Email & Marketing:** Hooking up Ghost to ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or CRM workflows.
*   **Custom API & Webhooks:** Building automations via Zapier, Make, or custom Node.js middleware for membership actions.

---

### 6. Ongoing Maintenance & Upgrades

Keep your self-hosted publication secure, up-to-date, and running without interruptions:

*   **Core & Theme Updates:** Safely upgrading Ghost core major versions and resolving theme compatibility errors.
*   **Security & Server Audits:** Managing OS upgrades, security patches, log rotation, and server resource monitoring.
*   **Emergency Fixes:** Promptly troubleshooting database issues, SSL expiration, crash recovery, and plugin/webhook failures.
